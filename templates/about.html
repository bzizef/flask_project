About This Dashboard

Personal Productivity Dashboard

A modern, feature-rich dashboard designed to help you stay organized and productive.

Key Features
  • To-Do List with progress tracking
  • Interactive Calendar for events
  • Pomodoro Timer for focused work sessions
  • Notes & Journal for ideas
  • Weather widget for your location
  • Productivity statistics & charts
  • CSV Export - Export Tasks, Notes, and Calendar events to CSV files
  • About Page - View project information and team details

Database

Frontend

Bootstrap 5.3

Charts
